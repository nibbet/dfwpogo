# Into the Wild 2025  
**Date:** November 10 – 14, 2025  
**Time:** 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM (local time)  
**Type:** Global Event  
**Theme:** Dark & Fairy Types  

Step into the Wild and get ready for the GO Wild Area 2025!  
Enjoy boosted spawns, themed raids, research rewards, and new trainer items.  


### Event Bonuses  
- **Remote Raid limit:** 30 per day (Nov 10–14)  
- **No Remote Raid limit:** Nov 15–16  
- **Frustration** can be removed from Shadow Pokémon with a Charged TM  
- **Special raid background** on caught Pokémon  
- **Event stickers** in PokéStops, Gifts, and Shop  


### Featured Spawns   
- Alolan Rattata (✨), Sableye (✨), Carvanha (✨), Purrloin (✨), Cottonee (✨)  
- Scraggy (✨), Spritzee (✨), Swirlix (✨), Carbink, Nickit, Fidough

### Timed Research — *Ready for the Wild*  

**Availability:** November 10 @ 10:00 AM – November 14 @ 6:00 PM (local)  
**Ticket Cost:** $4.99 USD (or local equivalent)  

Complete tasks to earn premium items and encounters with special Dynamax Pokémon.  


#### Step 1  
- Catch 20 Pokémon  
- Spin 10 PokéStops or Gyms  
- Win a Raid  

**Rewards:** Rare Candy ×2, 3,000 XP, 1,000 Stardust  


#### Step 2  
- Catch 20 Pokémon  
- Spin 10 PokéStops or Gyms  
- Power up Pokémon 10 times  
- Win a Max Battle  

**Rewards:** Encounter with Alolan Rattata (✨), Premium Battle Pass ×1, 2,000 Stardust  


#### Step 3  
- Use 3 supereffective Charged Attacks  
- Power up Pokémon 10 times  
- Unlock or use 3 Max Moves  
- Use Charged TM ×3  

**Rewards:** Max Mushroom ×1, Charged TM ×1, 3,000 XP  


#### Step 4  
- Catch 5 Fairy-type Pokémon  
- Power up Fairy-type Pokémon 5 times  
- Evolve 3 Fairy-type Pokémon  

**Rewards:** Dynamax Ralts encounter (✨), Premium Battle Pass ×1, Rare Candy XL ×1  


#### Step 5  
- Catch 5 Dark-type Pokémon  
- Power up Dark-type Pokémon 5 times  
- Evolve 3 Dark-type Pokémon  

**Rewards:** Dynamax Sableye encounter (✨), Rare Candy XL ×1, 5,000 XP  


**Completion Bonus:**  
- 5,000 Stardust  
- Max Mushroom ×1  
- Dynamax Ralts encounter (✨)
