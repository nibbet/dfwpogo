## Into the Wild 2025  
**Date:** November 10 – 14, 2025  
**Time:** 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM (local time)  
**Type:** Global Event  
**Theme:** Dark and Fairy Types  

Embark on the path to the **GO Wild Area 2025** with special spawns, research, raids, and exclusive rewards.
- Increased **Remote Raid limit (30 per day)**  
- **Frustration can be removed** from Shadow Pokémon via Charged TM  
- **Event stickers** available from PokéStops, Gifts, and Shop  
- Ticketed Timed Research ***“Ready for the Wild”*** 
  - Encounters with Dynamax Ralts and Sableye  
  - Max Mushroom items  
  - Premium Battle Passes and TMs  
- **GO Pass / GO Pass Deluxe — Into the Wild** 
  - Hyper Training access  
  - Debut of **Silver Bottle Cap** (Deluxe Track)  
  - Exclusive avatar items  
- **Field Research & Rewards**
  - Wild Area-themed tasks  
  - Encounters with event Pokémon  
  - Bonus XP and Stardust rewards
