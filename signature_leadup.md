# Signature Road Events — November 10–14, 2025  

## Into the Wild 2025  
**Date:** November 10 – 14, 2025  
**Time:** 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM (local time)  
**Type:** Global Event  
**Theme:** Dark & Fairy Types  

Embark on the path to the **GO Wild Area 2025** with themed spawns, raids, research, and unique bonuses leading up to the main global event.  

- **Remote Raid limit increased** to 30 per day  
- **Frustration can be removed** from Shadow Pokémon using a Charged TM  
- **Event stickers** available from PokéStops, Gifts, and the in-game Shop  
- **No Remote Raid limit** on November 15–16 during the GO Wild Area 2025 event
