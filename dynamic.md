# 🌍 Pokémon GO Wild Area: Global (2025)

**🗓️ Event Dates**  
**Starts:** Saturday, November 15, 2025 — 10:00 AM local time  
**Ends:** Sunday, November 16, 2025 — 6:00 PM local time  

**🎟️ Ticket Price:** $11.99 USD  
Available in the in-game shop and Pokémon GO Web Store.  
Purchase on the **Web Store** to receive a **Flower Crown avatar item**!

> “For the brave, adventure awaits around every corner—are you ready to venture into the Wild Area?”

---

## 🌐 Overview

Trainers worldwide can embark on a massive two-day adventure during **Pokémon GO Wild Area: Global**!  
Face **Gigantamax Grimmsnarl**, encounter new Pokémon, and enjoy massive bonuses throughout the weekend.

---

## ⚡ General Bonuses (All Trainers)
Available between 10:00 a.m.–6:00 p.m. local time on both days:
- Up to **3 free Raid Passes** per day from Gyms  
- **Daily Max Particle limit** increased to 1,600  
- **3 GO Snapshot** surprise encounters per day  

---

## 🎟️ Ticketed Bonuses
Available for ticket holders only (10:00 a.m.–6:00 p.m. local time):
- Increased chance of encountering **Shiny Pokémon** ✨  
- **Daily Max Particle limit:** 3,200  
- **More Max Particles** from Power Spots and exploration  
- **¾ Max Particle cost** for Max Moves  
- Up to **6 free Raid Passes** per day from Gyms  
- **2× Candy** for catching Pokémon  
- **½ Egg Hatch Distance** for Eggs placed during the event  
- **Lure Modules last 2 hours**  
- **6 Snapshot encounters** per day  

### 🌙 All-Day Ticketed Bonuses (12:00 a.m.–11:59 p.m.)
- Up to **6 Special Trades per day**  
- **½ Stardust cost** for trades

## 💥 Event Features

### 🌑 Gigantamax Grimmsnarl Debut
- Appears globally in **6-Star Max Battles**
- Complete the event-exclusive **Special Research** to encounter one!

### 👿 New Pokémon Debuts
- **Impidimp**, **Morgrem**, and **Grimmsnarl** make their Pokémon GO debuts!  
  - 25 Candy → evolve Impidimp → Morgrem  
  - 100 Candy → evolve Morgrem → Grimmsnarl

### 🚀 Shadow Raids
- **Saturday:** Shadow Cresselia (✨ Shiny available)  
- **Sunday:** Shadow Darkrai (✨ Shiny available — debut!)  

### 🐻‍❄️ Bewear (Wilderness Cape)
- Appears in **3-Star Raids** with a new wilderness outfit  

### 💪 Mighty Pokémon Encounters
- Stronger opponents with “!!!” instead of CP  
- More likely to be **XL/XXL** and **Shiny**  
- Capture more easily with **GO Safari Balls**  
- Different mighty Pokémon appear each day
