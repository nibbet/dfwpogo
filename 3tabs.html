ℹ️ Event Info & Tips

  • 🔋 Bring a power bank for your phone.
  • 💧 Stay hydrated – water refill stations are available.
  • 🌤️ Dress for the weather and wear comfy shoes.
  • 📱 Make sure your game is updated before arriving.
  • 📍 Use the Campfire app or Discord to communicate in real time.
  • 📸 Capture your favorite moments and share them with the group.

📋 Meetup Locations for Go Fest

We are hosting in two locations for each day of the event. You can join us at either location, or both!

We will be using the Campfire app to coordinate meetups and activities. Make sure to join our community there!

💫 Bonuses for All Trainers
  • Surprise encounters in GO Snapshot—when taking snapshots during the event, you never know who might show up!
  • One-hour Lure Module duration
  • Party Play duration increased from three hours to eight hours
🎫 All Day Ticket Bonuses
  • Buddy will gain more hearts for all friendship activities
  • Up to 9 free Raid Passes per day by spinning Photo Discs at Gyms
  • Open up to 40 Gifts per day
  • Up to 6 Special Trades per day
  • 1/2 Stardust Cost for Trades
🎟️ 10AM - 6PM Day Ticket Bonuses
  • Increased chance of encountering Shiny Pokémon
  • Special Research leading to an encounter with Volcanion
  • Special Research with branching paths: Sword or Shield
  • Different Pokémon will be attracted to Incense
  • Hourly Global Challenges with exciting rewards (Saturday and Sunday)
  • Habitat-themed Collection Challenges
  • Three surprise encounters in GO Snapshot per day
